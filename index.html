03 — What's Included

Everything in
The Protocol

This is not a template program. Every deliverable is built from your data. No generic PDFs. No weekly motivation check-ins disguised as coaching.

01 — Assessment

Calibration Assessment Report

A full-system audit of your current state. Biometrics, bloodwork flags, lifestyle constraints, eating patterns, training history, sleep architecture. Delivered as a premium formatted report before Week 1 begins. This is your baseline document and the reference point for every decision made over the next 12 weeks.

02 — Nutrition

Custom Nutrition Protocol

Built around your actual food environment, not a theoretical clean-eating template. Calorie and protein targets, meal structure, eating-out rules for Chennai and Bangalore restaurants, weekend deviation allowances, and IF/THEN adjustment triggers. Works whether you eat at a corporate canteen, a QSR, or cook at home.

03 — Training

Training Program (4-Week Blocks)

Periodised resistance training designed for your equipment, schedule, and training age. Progressive overload is built in. Substitution tables for when equipment is unavailable. Fatigue management protocols so you don't accumulate debt that kills consistency in Week 6.

04 — Analysis

Weekly Check-In Analysis

Every week: you submit your data, I analyse it. Weight trends, adherence breakdown, training quality, biofeedback scores. You get a written analysis, a root cause diagnosis, and specific protocol adjustments within 24 hours. Not a template reply. An actual decision with reasoning attached.

05 — Support

WhatsApp Support (Weekdays)

Direct access. Response within 4 hours on weekdays. Not a community group chat. Not a support ticket system. One coach, one client, focused on your current protocol state. Questions answered with reasoning, not reassurance. Escalations handled fast.

06 — Bloodwork

Monthly Bloodwork Review

If you have labs, I review them in the context of your protocol. HbA1c, fasting insulin, lipids, thyroid, Vitamin D, ferritin, and testosterone for male clients. Flags are noted. Protocol adjustments are made where evidence supports it. Medical referrals recommended where necessary, without diagnosing.

07 — Quarterly

Re-Calibration Audit (Q Plan)

Every 12 weeks: full system reset. Review what worked, what didn't, and why. New baselines established. Protocol updated for the next quarter. The mechanism that prevents you from hitting a ceiling after the first three months of good results.

08 — Reports

Monthly Progress Reports

Data-rich formatted reports showing your progress trajectory. Trend analysis, comparison against targets, protocol performance summary. Built to show your numbers accurately, not to make you feel good about a bad month. Honesty is a feature, not a problem.

Note

Maximum 5 new clients accepted per quarter. This is not a marketing constraint. It is a quality constraint. Weekly analysis per client takes 30 to 60 minutes of focused work. WhatsApp support requires real response quality. More than 5 new clients makes that impossible to sustain at the standard this protocol demands.

07 — Frequently Asked

Common Questions

How is this different from hiring a regular personal trainer? +
A personal trainer is present during sessions and gives you exercises to do. The Calibration Protocol is a full system that covers nutrition, training, recovery, bloodwork, weekly data analysis, protocol adjustments, and exit planning. The coaching is continuous and data-driven, not session-based. You are not paying for Guhay's time. You are paying for the system and its outputs.
Do I need to be in Chennai or Bangalore? +
No. The protocol is fully online. All communication is via WhatsApp and email. Check-ins are submitted through a structured form. The nutrition protocols are built for Indian food environments including QSR, corporate canteens, and home cooking. Clients in Hyderabad, Mumbai, and Delhi have been onboarded without any issues.
I work a demanding job. Can I actually follow this? +
Yes. This protocol was built specifically for people working 10-hour days. The nutrition system does not require meal prepping or cooking. The training programs are designed for 3 to 4 days per week with sessions under 60 minutes. The check-in format takes approximately 5 minutes per week. The protocol adapts to your constraints, not the other way around.
What results can I expect? +
The average client sees 6 to 10 kg of measurable fat loss across a 12-week protocol with full adherence. Results depend on your starting state, adherence percentage, and constraints. There is no guarantee of a specific outcome because physiology has variables outside both our control. What is guaranteed: every protocol adjustment will be data-driven and every decision will be explained. Zero clients have completed a full protocol without measurable changes.
What if I miss a week or go off-plan? +
Deviation is expected and built into the system. The protocol includes re-calibration triggers for when adherence drops. If you miss a check-in, a follow-up message goes out the same day. If a work trip or festival period disrupts the plan, the protocol is adjusted for that week. No guilt. No lectures. Data and a correction.
Why only 5 clients per quarter? +
Weekly check-in analysis for each client takes 30 to 60 minutes of focused work. WhatsApp support requires real-time response quality. More than 5 new clients per quarter means the analysis quality drops or response times increase. Neither is acceptable at this price point. The cap is a quality constraint, not a marketing tactic.
Is there a refund if it doesn't work? +
There is no refund policy after the assessment report is delivered and Week 1 begins. The work invested in your baseline analysis, custom protocols, and first check-in analysis is not recoverable. This is why there is an application process and a 20-minute diagnostic call before onboarding. If the fit is not right, that gets identified before payment. After it begins, the commitment is mutual.
